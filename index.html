Why another media player? And why OpenPlayer?

  • Video
  • Audio

Technologies are evolving at fast pace. Having a player that plays the most popular content in all browsers and devices, keeps itself ever-green, and can be customized easily is a no-brainer for everyone that wants the best.

Easily configured

Either setting up the player through the video/audio tags or via Javascript, you just need a pair of class names and up to 3 configuration options... and you are all set!

Smart Autoplay

Giving the constant changes of policies on different browsers, OpenPlayer integrates a smart algorithm to detect autoplay capabilities so you don't have to worry about each browser's quirks or workarounds for that.

VAST/VPAID/VMAP support

OpenPlayer integrates Google IMA SDK so users have full support for all the types of specifications for Ads in the current market. Just provide a valid URL and let OpenPlayer to take care of the rest!
